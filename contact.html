Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to post a job?

Job posting costs a fixed fee of R100 for 30 days of visibility. No hidden costs or monthly subscriptions.

How quickly will my job posting go live?

Your job posting goes live immediately after payment confirmation. No waiting for approval processes.

Can I edit my job posting after it's live?

Yes, you can edit your job posting at any time during the 30-day period by contacting our support team.

Is it free for job seekers to apply?

Absolutely! Job seekers can browse and apply to jobs completely free. We never charge candidates.

What file formats can I upload for my CV?

We accept PDF, DOC, and DOCX files up to 5MB in size for CV uploads.

How do I receive applications for my job posting?

Applications are sent directly to the email address you provide when posting the job. You'll receive instant notifications.
